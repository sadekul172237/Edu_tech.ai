The 6-stroke engine is a revolutionary advancement in internal combustion engine design. Unlike traditional 4-stroke engines, the 6-stroke engine adds two additional strokes to improve fuel efficiency, reduce emissions, and optimize thermal performance.

Researchers and engineering institutions around the world are actively studying various 6-stroke engine prototypes. In 2025, institutions such as MIT (USA), IITs (India), and several European universities are focusing on:

  • Water injection as the fifth and sixth strokes for steam expansion.
  • Secondary air or exhaust strokes for emission control.
  • Hybrid integration with electric systems for enhanced performance.
  • Fuel economy improvement by up to 40% compared to traditional engines.
